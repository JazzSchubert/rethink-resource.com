Our Story

RethinkResource was founded by Linda Grieder in 2016 with the mission to support the producing industry in the transition to a closed loop economy. Linda saw the strategic impact that digitalisation will have on the production systems of the future. She saw digital value networks instead of linear supply chains, value instead of waste, sustainable value creation instead of exploitation.

The first step was the creation of a consulting business to bring the best upcycling innovations from across the world to the Swiss Food Industry and beyond. From our office in Technopark Zürich, we innovate on resource efficency in close collaboration with partners and customers from industry, academia, the startup ecosystem, and more. Our consultants scout new innovations, facilitate collaborations and develop business cases to champion upcycling and sustainability.

With the launch of Circado, our trading platform for industrial side streams, we layed the foundation for a European Circular Economy in the form of a digital hub for industry transformation. The automisation of the exchange of side streams enables scalability of secondary sourcing and paves the way to the mainstream for existing and future upcycling innovations.

At RethinkResource, we continue the journey to the circular economy with passion and commitment. If you feel inspired by our mission or just have some questions, please feel free to contact us here.
